### --- Features --- ###
D&D style game
  "Hijack" (http://thecommentsection.org/viewtopic.php?f=42&t=1436)

Hot potato game ("Hot Potato Grenade")
  Get list of people playing
  Pass potato to random player
  Two timers:
    Individual timer for player to pass (!pass [player]) to another (5 seconds)
    Overall timer is as long as it takes to sing "Hot Potato" (20 seconds?)
  Potato explodes on current player holding it when a timer runs out.

Custom greetings and farewells
  - CleverDick:
    - %nick% pulled out. (f)
  - Jackroad:
    - Highway (g)

Mail feature
  - !tell [nick] [message]
  - Messages the user once they log in
  - "A message from [nick]: [message] <[date]>"
